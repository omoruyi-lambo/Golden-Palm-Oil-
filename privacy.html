Privacy Policy

How Golden Palm handles website inquiries and contact details.

Last updated on March 8, 2026. This page explains what information is collected through the site, how it is used, and how you can contact us if you want your details corrected or removed.

Quick summary

We only ask for information needed to respond to sales or partnership inquiries.

  • Contact data is used to reply to your request.
  • We do not sell inquiry information to third parties.
  • You can request deletion or correction by email.

Effective date: March 8, 2026

1. Information we collect

When you contact Golden Palm through the website, email, or phone, we may collect your name, business name, email address, phone number, order interest, delivery needs, and any message you choose to send.

2. How we use your information

We use inquiry information to respond to quote requests, share product availability, discuss delivery timelines, confirm orders, and improve communication with potential or returning buyers.

3. Legal basis for use

Information is processed because it is necessary to respond to your request, support a commercial relationship, or meet record-keeping obligations tied to product supply and client communication.

4. How long we keep information

Inquiry records are retained only for as long as needed to answer your request, maintain a business relationship, or document completed transactions. Records may be removed earlier if you request deletion and there is no legal reason to keep them.

5. Sharing with third parties

We do not sell personal information. Limited details may be shared with service providers involved in communication, delivery coordination, or bookkeeping when that sharing is necessary to complete a legitimate business process.

6. Cookies and analytics

This website is designed as a lightweight static site and does not rely on heavy tracking tools. Basic technical logs may still be created by your browser, hosting platform, or web server in the normal course of delivering the site.

7. Data security

Reasonable administrative and technical measures are used to protect inquiry information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. No system can guarantee absolute security, so you should avoid sending unnecessary sensitive information through open email.

8. Your choices

You may request access to the personal information we hold about you, ask us to correct inaccurate details, or request deletion where appropriate. Send requests to info@goldenpalmoil.com.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, email info@goldenpalmoil.com or call +234 800 123 4567.
